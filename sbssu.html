About Us

College Introduction

SARDAR BEANT SINGH STATE UNIVERSITY of Technology was established in 1995 with the aim of providing quality technical education. We are affiliated with SBSSU and approved by AICTE.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To impart quality education and produce skilled professionals who can contribute to society.

Vision: To become a center of excellence in technical education and research.

Principal Message

"At SBSSU College, we believe in holistic development of students. Our focus is not just on academics but also on character building and practical skills. Welcome to our family."

- Dr. R.K. Sharma, Principal

College History

Founded in 1995 by the SBSSU Educational Trust, the college started with 60 students and 3 courses. Today we have 2000+ students and 15+ courses across UG and PG levels.
